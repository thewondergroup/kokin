01 The Experience

A long, easy lunch with plenty to drink.

Saturdays and Sundays at Kokin belong to the long lunch. A fixed three-course menu pulls from across the kitchen — fried, raw, and fire-kissed — paired with ninety minutes of free-flowing drinks.

Small plates to start, signature TEMAE sushi made with smoked rice, a finisher of wood-fire smoked ice cream. Drink through it at your own pace.

05 FAQ
Can you accommodate dietary requirements?

Yes — please let us know in advance when booking. Some dishes can be adapted; others are difficult to substitute (the TEMAE sushi, for example, is built around specific tuna cuts). We'll always do our best to work around what you need.

What's your cancellation policy?

Bookings can be amended or cancelled up to 24 hours in advance at no charge. Inside 24 hours, a cancellation fee may apply. Email reservations@kokin.co.uk if you need to change your booking.

Can I book brunch for a private group?

Yes. We take private group bookings of 8 or more for brunch service — get in touch at reservations@kokin.co.uk with your preferred date and party size.
